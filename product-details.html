    ARC 250

    The Arc Welder ARC 250 is a powerful, efficient welding machine with 250A output, ideal for industrial and heavy-duty welding applications.

    TIG - 200 AC /DC Features

    • Multi - Functional : Pulse TIG , AC /DC and MMA Function.
    • 2T / 4T Function.
    • Down- Slope : 0 - 15 S Adjustable.
    • Post Flow 0 - 10 S Adjustable.
    • Pluse Frequency 0 - 5HZ Adjustable
    • Wide Range for Input Voltage : 220V ± 15 %

    Applications

    • Aluminum Products
    • Decoration
    • Light & Medium Fabrication
    • Pipes & High Pressure Vessels
    • Construction
